Labs

01

 02

 03

Conflict

Meet a merge conflict and resolve it calmly.

 04

Replay commits and keep history readable.

 05

Undo

Restore, amend, reset, and revert with intent.

 06

Push a branch and open a clean pull request.

 07

Team workflow

Ignore junk, sync often, write reviewable PRs.
